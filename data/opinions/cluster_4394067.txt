PER CURIAM.

       Minnesota resident Dennis Deters petitions this Court for review after the
Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) denied him a medical certificate needed to
re-establish his private pilot’s license. He has also filed a motion to supplement the
record.
      We have reviewed the record and the parties’ arguments, and we conclude that
the FAA’s decision was not arbitrary, capricious, an abuse of discretion, or without
support in the law. See Reder v. Adm’r of FAA, 116 F.3d 1261, 1263 (8th Cir. 1997)
(standard of review). We deny the petition for review and deny as moot Deters’s
motion to supplement the record.
