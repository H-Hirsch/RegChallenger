        PER CURIAM:

              Scott Webber appeals from the tax court’s orders granting summary judgment in

        favor of the Commissioner and allowing the proposed levy action to proceed with respect

        to Webber’s 2013 income tax liability and denying Webber’s motion to alter or amend or

        to vacate the order.    We have reviewed the record and find no reversible error.

        Accordingly, we affirm the tax court’s orders. Webber v. Comm’r of Internal Revenue,

        Tax Ct. No. 14307-18L (U.S. Tax Ct. May 22, 2023; Aug. 25, 2023). We dispense with

        oral argument because the facts and legal contentions are adequately presented in the

        materials before this court and argument would not aid the decisional process.

                                                                                     AFFIRMED
