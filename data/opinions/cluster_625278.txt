Before JOLLY, DAVIS, and BARKSDALE, Circuit Judges.
PER CURIAM:*
        After reviewing the record and considering the briefs of the parties and the
arguments of counsel, we are satisfied that substantial evidence supports the
Occupational Safety and Health Review Commission’s (“OSHRC”) finding that
petitioner violated the two OSHA standards at issue in this appeal. We also find
no error in the OSHRC’s legal conclusions. Accordingly, the petition for review
from the May 19, 2011 OSHRC order is DENIED.
