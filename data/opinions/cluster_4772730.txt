                         ORDER

    In light of the Supreme Court’s decision in Department
of Homeland Security v. Regents of the University of
California, 140 S. Ct. 1891 (2020), we remand these cases
to the district court for further proceedings consistent with
the Supreme Court’s opinion, which held that the
Department of Homeland Security’s rescission of the
Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals program “was
arbitrary and capricious in violation of the [Administrative
Procedure Act],” id. at 1915.

    IT IS SO ORDERED.            The mandate shall issue
forthwith.
