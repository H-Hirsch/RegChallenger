                         ORDER

    The Supreme Court reversed our judgment denying
Petitioner’s petition for review. See City & County of San
Francisco v. EPA, 145 S. Ct. 704, 720 (2025). In accordance
with the judgment of the Supreme Court, the petition for
review is granted, Section I.I.1 of Attachment G and Section
V of the challenged permit are vacated, and the matter is
remanded to the Environmental Protection Agency for
further proceedings consistent with the opinion of the
Supreme Court.
   IT IS SO ORDERED.
