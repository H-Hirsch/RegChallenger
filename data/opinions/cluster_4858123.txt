PER CURIAM:

       Winfred F. Nicholson appeals from the tax court’s order dismissing his tax court

petition for lack of jurisdiction. We have reviewed the record and find no reversible error.

Accordingly, we affirm for the reasons stated by the tax court. Nicholson v. Comm’r of

Internal Rev., Tax Ct. No. 001380-20 L (U.S. Tax Ct. Aug. 10, 2020). We dispense with

oral argument because the facts and legal contentions are adequately presented in the

materials before this court and argument would not aid the decisional process.

                                                                               AFFIRMED
